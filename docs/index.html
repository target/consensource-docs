Certification Transparency and Authenticity using Blockchain Technology

ConsenSource is designed to leverage permissioned Distributed Ledger Technolgy (Blockchain) to bring transparency and trust to the sourcing industry - making it easier to find and verify the certifications and audit histories of a factory.

Trusted

The immutable and distributed nature of the blockchain provides verifiable trust that all transactions on the network are authentic.

Transparent

Certification records and audit history are broadcasted and agreed upon by all members of ConsenSource.

Permissioned

The ConsenSource governing body controls permissioning of all actors of the network, limiting access to only approved and relevant organizations..
